01 — The Problem

EGYPT'S INDUSTRY
RUNS ON HEAT.

Every cement factory, every ceramics plant, every food processing line depends on high-temperature thermal energy to operate. And today, almost all of that heat comes from natural gas and diesel fuel.

Since global fuel price shocks, factories absorb these costs with no hedge, no backup, and no alternative. Energy bills now represent up to 40% of total operating costs.

"Egypt receives some of the highest solar radiation on Earth — yet that energy falls on the same deserts where our industrial cities are built, and goes completely to waste. Every. Single. Day."

02 — The Solution

WE STOPPED LOOKING
DOWN FOR FUEL.

ATON captures solar heat, stores it inside a sand-based thermal battery, and delivers it directly to factories as a clean, stable, and affordable alternative to gas and diesel.

Factories don't buy equipment. They don't sign fuel contracts. They subscribe to heat at a fixed, lower cost — and we handle everything.

No price shocks. No carbon. No dependency on anyone.

HEAT-AS-A-SERVICE The sun does the work. We do the delivery.
☀ CAPTURE
Parabolic solar mirrors concentrate sunlight onto the storage unit

Sand thermal battery stores heat for 12–18 hours
